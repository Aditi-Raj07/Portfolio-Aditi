### 1. Interval – AI-Powered Mock Interview Platform
*Individual Project*
- **Description:** Designed a conversational AI simulation tool facilitating Technical, Core, HR, and Rapid Fire mock interview settings with adjustable constraints.
- **Architecture:** Integrated **Groq's LLaMA model** via targeted prompt engineering to deliver adaptive, context-aware querying and detailed analytical text responses.

*SIH 2025 Finalist | Team Project*
